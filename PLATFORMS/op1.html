Uber Eats
Uber Eats, an extension of Uber, is a premier online food delivery platform connecting restaurants with a wide customer base. Through its user-friendly app and website, Uber Eats streamlines the ordering process, offering restaurants valuable insights for optimizing their services in today's fast-paced digital dining landscape.

Link: https://www.ubereats.com/

Source: https://nextbite.io/get-your-restaurant-on-ubereats/
STEPS:
1. Fill out the Interest Form:
- Begin by completing an interest form on the Uber Eats website. Provide essential details about your restaurant, including your name, contact information, restaurant name and address, number of locations, cuisine type, estimated weekly to-go orders, and information about your delivery staff.

2. Submission:
- Submit the filled interest form through the Uber Eats website.

3. Wait for Response:
- The Uber Eats team will review your application and respond within one to three business days.

4. Acceptance Notification:
- You'll receive notification regarding the acceptance or status of your application. This communication will detail whether your restaurant has been accepted to join Uber Eats.

ZOMATO
Zomato, a leading online food ordering and discovery platform, has transformed the Indian food industry. A go-to app for millions, it offers restaurant discovery and online food ordering services. For restaurant owners in India, registering on Zomato is a key strategy to enhance visibility and reach a broader audience.

Link: https://www.zomato.com/

Source: https://blog.petpooja.com/restaurant-zomato-registration/
STEPS:
1. Create an account on Zomato:
- Visit the Zomato website or download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
- Create an account using your email ID or mobile number.

2. Add your restaurant details:
- Fill out the registration form with accurate information.
- Include your restaurant name, location, cuisine type, opening hours, and contact details.

3. Verification code:
- Zomato will send a verification code to your registered phone number or email ID.
- Enter the code to verify your restaurant’s details and activate your account.

4. Add menu items & photos:
- After verification, add your menu items and high-quality photos showcasing your dishes.
- Allow customers to browse your menu and experience your restaurant's ambiance.

5. Zomato’s verification:
- A Zomato representative will contact you after submitting the registration form.
- Your restaurant will go live within a week, and a Point of Contact will be assigned for any queries.

6. Manage your restaurant’s profile:
- Regularly update your profile by adding new menu items and photos.
- Respond to customer reviews and feedback, and promote your restaurant’s events and offers.

SWIGGY
Swiggy is a prominent food delivery platform connecting restaurants with customers. Their partner program offers a significant opportunity for restaurant owners to broaden their customer base. With Swiggy being a top-of-mind choice for food delivery, joining the program can be a gamechanger for restaurant owners seeking expansion.

Link: https://www.swiggy.com

Source: https://restaurant.store/partner-with-swiggy-a-complete-guide/
STEPS:
1. Go to the official website - swiggy.com:
- On the footer, find and click on the option “Partner with Us”.

2. Fill out the initial details:
- Restaurant name
- Owner name
- Restaurant POC designation (Owner/Manager)
- Owner contact number
- Owner's email ID
- City of the restaurant's location

3. Submit your application:
- Click the submit button. A new box will appear for additional information.

4. Provide more details:
- If adding more details online, provide:
- Website link/online listing link
- Number of outlets
- Type of cuisine
- Cost for two
- Type of establishment
- Delivery support (yes or no)

5. Attach required documents:
- Shop License
- FSSAI (Food Safety License)
- GSTIN/PAN

6. Attach pictures:
- Facade
- Kitchen
- Dining/Packaging
- Locality

7. Submit your application:
- Press submit to complete your registration; you will receive a notification.

8. Keep hard copies of documents ready:
- Swiggy officials will reach out for further processes and the Swiggy restaurant partner agreement.

Note: It takes 2 or 3 weeks for the tie-up process to be completed and receive final approval.
